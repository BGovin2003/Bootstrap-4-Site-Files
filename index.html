Other Interesting facts about my family!

My parents are originally from South Africa but my grandfather was from India. My grandfather owned a rice mill and my dad would travel back and forth. He would help at the mill in India on holidays and then travel back to South Africa for school.

My mom and dad became close after my mom tutored him in a language and they got along really well. They lost touch after high school, but they reunited and decided to see if they really liked each other.
